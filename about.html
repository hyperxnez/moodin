About Moodin

Discover places that match your emotions. We're reimagining how people explore their cities.

Our Mission

At Moodin, we believe that the places you visit should reflect how you feel. Traditional discovery apps show you what's popular or nearby, but we show you what matches your current mood.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading emotion-driven discovery platform, helping millions of people find joy, comfort, excitement, or peace in their everyday surroundings.

Our Values

1

User-Centric

Every feature we build starts with understanding our users' emotional needs and daily experiences.

2

Privacy First

Your mood data is personal. We never share or sell your information to third parties.

3

Inclusive Discovery

We believe everyone deserves to find places that resonate with them, regardless of budget or location.

4

Continuous Innovation

We're constantly improving our mood detection algorithms and discovery features based on user feedback.
