Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does Moodin's mood-based discovery work?

Moodin uses a proprietary algorithm that matches your current emotional state with venue attributes like atmosphere, music, lighting, crowd energy, and user reviews. When you check in with your mood, we analyze patterns from millions of user interactions to suggest places that historically match your vibe.

Is the free version really free? What are the limitations?

Yes! The free version is completely free forever, no credit card required. You get unlimited mood check-ins, personalized recommendations, and basic booking features. Premium ($4.99/mo) adds unlimited collections, ad-free browsing, priority bookings, and weekly mood insights.

How do you protect my data and privacy?

We take privacy seriously. Your mood data is encrypted and never shared with third parties without explicit consent. You can export or delete your data anytime. We comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations. Partner venues only see aggregate anonymized data.

How can I become a partner venue?

Join our partner program for free! No monthly fees—we only charge a small commission on bookings made through Moodin. You'll get access to our dashboard with visitor insights, promotional tools, and mood-targeted marketing options. Apply at moodin.app/partners.

What if I need help or support?

We offer 24/7 in-app chat support for Premium users and email support for free users (typically <24hr response time). Visit our Help Center at help.moodin.app or email support@moodin.app. We also have an active community forum.
